About

Jazmin has always had a love for music. She played the clarinet in the 6th grade and began taking piano lessons in the 8th grade. In high school, Jazmin produced her first song entitled, "Champs Anthem" to celebrate her high school (Columbus Africentric Early College) state championship win! This was the beginning of a passion for production within Jazmin's spirit.

Talk about Columbus Teen Idol here also.


Education

After 5 years of piano lessons Jazmin graduated high school and began college in 2012. Jazmin studies computer science and engineering at The Ohio State University. She will be graduating in Spring of 2016 with her BS in CSE.


Work
