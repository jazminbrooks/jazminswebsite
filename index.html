About

Jazmin (born and raised in Columbus, Ohio) has always had a love for music. She played the clarinet in the 6th grade and began taking piano lessons in the 8th grade. In high school, Jazmin produced her first song entitled, "Champs Anthem" to celebrate her high school (Columbus Africentric Early College) state championship win! This was when Jazmin discovered her passion for music production.

At the age of 15, Jazmin created Columbus Teen Idol. Columbus Teen Idol provided a platform for youth (ages 13-19) to gain performance experience while competing for cash prizes and a free demo recording session. Throughout high school, Jazmin put on 4 highly successful Columbus Teen Idol events.


Education

After 5 years of piano lessons Jazmin graduated high school and began college in 2012. Jazmin currently studies computer science and engineering at The Ohio State University. She will be graduating in Spring of 2016 with her BS in CSE.


Work

Jazmin Brooks works as a manager for The Ohio State University Women's Basketball team during the school year. She is also an extended IT intern at Humana, Inc.
