Background

London "LX" Elixir (born and raised in Columbus, Ohio) has always had a love for music. She played the clarinet in the 6th grade and began taking piano lessons in the 8th grade. In high school, London Elixir produced her first song entitled, "Champs Anthem" to celebrate her high school (Columbus Africentric Early College) state championship win! This was when London Elixir discovered her passion for music production.

Columbus Teen Idol

At the age of 15, London Elixir created Columbus Teen Idol. Columbus Teen Idol provided a platform for youth (ages 13-19) to gain performance experience while competing for cash prizes and a free demo recording session. Throughout high school, London Elixir put on 4 highly successful Columbus Teen Idol events.

Education

London Elixir studied computer science and engineering at The Ohio State University. She graduated in Spring of 2016 with her BS in CSE. A couple weeks prior to graduating from Ohio State, she decided to enroll in music production school so that she could pursue her passion for music.
